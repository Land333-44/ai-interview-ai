# Design Prompt: Smart Coach AI - Get Started Screen

**Target:** Modern Mobile Onboarding Screen
**App Name:** Smart Coach AI
**Style:** Clean, futuristic, minimalist. Inspired by Duolingo, Notion AI, and ChatGPT mobile UI.

## Visual Direction:
- **Color Palette:** Primary Sky Blue (#87CEEB), White (#FFFFFF), and Soft Light Gray/Purple accents. High contrast for readability.
- **Typography:** Professional Sans-serif (Manrope or similar). Main headline uses a mix of colors for emphasis.
- **Component Style:** Smooth rounded cards (20px+ corner radius), soft shadows for depth, and premium glassmorphism effects.

## Screen Layout:

### 1. Top Bar:
- **Logo/Brand:** "Smart Coach AI" with a modern AI/Lightbulb icon in Sky Blue.
- **Trailing Icon:** Help/Info icon (question mark).

### 2. Hero Section:
- **Illustration:** A futuristic AI assistant/robot chibi illustration in sky blue and purple gradients.
- **Background:** Subtle sound wave patterns and geometric floating elements to represent AI processing.

### 3. Messaging:
- **Main Title:** "Your Personal AI Communication Coach" (Centered, Bold).
- **Subtitle:** "Analyze your voice, emotions, and speaking style with real-time AI feedback." (Centered, secondary text color).

### 4. Feature Cards (2x2 Grid or Vertical Stack):
- **Card 1 (Voice Analysis):** Icon in soft blue circle. Text: "Analyze your voice, texte, image".
- **Card 2 (Emotion Detection):** Icon in soft gray circle. Text: "Detect your emotions - Understand the impact of your delivery."
- **Card 3 (AI Feedback):** Icon in soft purple circle. Text: "Get AI feedback - Personalized coaching suggestions delivered instantly after each session."
- **Card 4 (Skill Improvement):** Icon in soft blue circle. Text: "Improve your skills - Guided exercises designed to strengthen your vocal range and clarity."

### 5. Call to Action:
- **Primary Button:** Full-width "Get Started" button in Sky Blue with a right arrow icon.
- **Secondary Link:** "Already have an account? Sign In" (Centered text with 'Sign In' in Sky Blue).

## Technical Requirements:
- Responsive mobile-first design.
- Flutter-ready layout (8px grid system).
- Use of modern gradients and depth-based shadows.
